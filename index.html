Our Story

BORN FROM
PASSION

Vaishnavi Tiffin Centre opened its doors in 2025 with one simple mission — to serve hot, fresh, authentic South Indian breakfast that feels like home. Located in the heart of Kalaburagi on S B College Road, we've quickly become a trusted morning ritual for students, working professionals, and families alike.

Every item is prepared fresh each morning using quality ingredients. No shortcuts, no compromise — just good, honest food served with warmth.
