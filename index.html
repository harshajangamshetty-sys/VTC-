Our Story

Made with
Passion & Pride

Vaishnavi Tiffin Centre (VTC) opened its doors in 2025 with one simple mission — to bring hot, fresh, authentic South Indian breakfast to every table in Kalaburagi. Nestled on S B College Road, we've become a trusted morning ritual for students, families, and working professionals alike.

Every dish is prepared from scratch each morning using quality ingredients. No shortcuts, no compromise — just good, honest food made the traditional way and served with genuine warmth.
